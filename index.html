Our Story

Founded in 2010, RVG Properties has revolutionized the way people buy, sell, and rent properties in India. What started as a small team's vision to make real estate transparent and accessible has grown into India's largest property platform.

With over 50,000+ verified properties across 100+ cities, we've helped more than 1 lakh families find their perfect homes. Our commitment to transparency, technology, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for property seekers nationwide.
